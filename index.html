Role of Housing Market in Resource Allocation in General Equilibrium Research Interests: My primary research interests include:

1. International Business Cycles: Investigating the pivotal role of housing in shaping international business cycles.

2. International Finance : Examining the impact of liquidity demands and valuation effects in international financial markets on nominal exchange rate determination and cross-border wealth redistribution.

3. House Prices and Household Choices: Analyzing how house prices influence the life-cycle decisions of households, with a particular focus on investment decision and occupation choices.
